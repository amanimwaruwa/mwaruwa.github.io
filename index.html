History of football in england

Football in England has a long history, dating back to the 14th century. The late 19th century saw the rise of formal football competitions, with the oldest football competition in England being the Youdan Cup, played in 1867. The nature of folk football evolved into codification, with the promotion of football by the British Empire and public school football1. Sheffield, Cambridge and FA rules were established, with the impact of rule changes from 1863 to 1891. Competitive, international and professional football emerged from 1871 to 1890.
